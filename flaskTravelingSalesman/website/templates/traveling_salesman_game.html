How to play...

"The travelling salesman problem asks the following question: "Given a list of cities and the distances between each pair of cities, what is the shortest possible route that visits each city exactly once and returns to the origin city?""

The object of the game is to connect the dots and keep the distance traveled as low as possible. You get a higher score based on the time you take and the distance you use. See how far you can go!

Simply move your mouse on the grid and click between two dots to connect them. You must connect all the dots in a sequence. Make good decisions about space, direction, and distance. Good luck.
